Mission

The Wisdom Trust aims to create a wider awarness - a greater wisdom - about important issues, especially those affecting the future of our planet - poverty, environment & health, to make donations to support the work of others charities & good causes worldwide, and to help deliver better friendship, goodwill and understanding across all borders.
